Diet Coke Reviews

Below, I review Diet Coke quality at various restaurants. Seems like a good use of everyone’s time!

McDonalds, USA 🍔 🍟#

  • The gold standard; Diet Coke in its canonical form.
  • Seriously, there is no point in reading the rest of these reviews.
  • Why is it so good?

Feed Co., Redmond WA 🍔#

Editors note: This restaurant is now "3Bs Burger".

TL;DR

  • Fountain with average amount of syrup.
  • Glasses are way too small.
  • Seriously, I had to get up so many times for refills. I understand they’re trying to throttle my soda consumption which (1) won’t work and (2) just results in a bad user experience.

Otto sushi, Redmond WA 🍣#

TL;DR

  • Served in glass with ice.
  • Unlimited refills.
  • Decent amount of syrup, although they certainly don’t account for the ice melting (see: McDonalds review).

Danbo ramen, Capitol Hill in Seattle, WA 🍜#

TL;DR

  • Can with accompanying glass with ice.
  • Comes with straw.

Kizuki Ramen & Izakaya Bellevue, WA 🍜#

TL;DR

  • Good size glass, comes with straw.
  • Great amount of ice.
  • Syrup amount is significantly better than average.
  • Free refills.

Garlic crush, Redmond WA, 🥙#

TL;DR

  • Fountain with average amount of syrup.
  • Nothing special, but passable.

Village Square Cafe, Redmond, WA 🍳#

TL;DR

  • Very cool vintage Coke glass.
  • Good amount of ice.
  • Amount of syrup is average.

Chipotle Mexican Grill, Bellevue, WA, 🌯#

TL;DR

  • Standard fountain setup.
  • Free refills.
  • No complaints, solidly average.

Five Guys, Redmond, WA, 🍔#

TL;DR

  • Standard CocaCola Remix machine.
  • Paper cup is a bit flimsy.
  • Passable.

Stone, Korean BBQ, Redmond, WA#

TL;DR

  • Fairly watered down when I tried it.
  • Good ice.
  • I’d still order it if I went, but I won’t be excited about it.
